About ECEC

Building the next generation of electrical and computer engineers through collaboration, innovation, and community.

Our Mission

ECEC bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application. We empower students to design, build, and deploy cutting-edge projects across hardware, software, and embedded systems.

More than just technical excellence, we foster a vibrant community through workshops, hack nights, competitions, and social events.
