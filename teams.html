Design Teams

Collaborative squads tackling real-world engineering challenges across hardware, software, and embedded systems.

ECEC is organized into modular design teams, each focused on a specific domain. Teams work on competition entries, passion projects, and learning initiatives. New teams can be proposed by any member—innovation starts with you.

Robotics Collective

Active

Mission

Current Project

Focus Areas

  • Mechanical Design
  • Control Systems
  • Computer Vision
  • Embedded Firmware
